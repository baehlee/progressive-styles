Hudson, WI · Twin Cities Metro

Living in Hudson.

Hudson sits just across the St. Croix River in Wisconsin, about half an hour east of St. Paul — close enough to commute, far enough to feel like its own place. Its walkable downtown, riverfront park and beach, and small-town calendar make it a favorite for buyers who want river-town life with quick access to the Twin Cities.

It's also a relocation and second-home magnet. Plenty of metro families keep a foot on both sides of the river, and Wisconsin residency comes with its own lifestyle and tax considerations worth talking through. Whether you're chasing a riverfront lifestyle, a shorter commute to the east metro, or simply more home for the money, Hudson rewards a buyer who knows how to weigh the cross-border details.

What buyers should know.

The neighborhoods

Downtown Hudson offers historic homes and walkable charm near Lakefront Park and the beach, while newer subdivisions climb the bluffs with valley views. Along and near the river, you'll find everything from cottages to substantial waterfront homes.

Schools & getting around

The Hudson School District serves the area, and I-94 makes the run to downtown St. Paul and the east metro genuinely easy — one of the shortest "another state" commutes you'll find anywhere.

Parks & everyday life

The St. Croix is the centerpiece: boating, the beach, the riverfront park, and a downtown built for strolling. Add the seasonal festivals and a strong sense of community, and Hudson feels like a getaway you happen to live in.

The housing stock

Hudson mixes historic downtown homes, newer bluff-top subdivisions, and riverfront properties. River and view homes carry a premium, and crossing into Wisconsin changes the tax and lending picture — details we make sure you understand before you fall for a view.

Who Hudson fits

It's a strong match for east-metro commuters, river lovers, and families drawn to small-town life — plus second-home and cabin buyers. If the St. Croix is calling, Hudson is the place to answer.

Crossing the river: what to know

Buying in Hudson means buying in Wisconsin, and that changes a few things worth understanding up front — state taxes, lending nuances, and how river and view premiums work along the St. Croix. None of it is complicated with the right guidance, but it's exactly the kind of detail an out-of-area agent can miss.
