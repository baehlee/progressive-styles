Questions we hear a lot

¿Hablan español de verdad? / Do you really speak Spanish?

Sí. Rodolfo is a fluent, native-level Spanish speaker who works with Spanish-speaking families every week — not an agent who hands you off to a translator. You can call, text, and review your documents with someone who speaks your language.

¿Trabajan con vendedores también? / Do you help sellers too?

Sí. Whether you're buying your first home or selling the one you've outgrown, we guide you through it in your language, with strategic pricing and honest advice at every step.

¿Qué documentos necesito? / What documents will I need?

Generally proof of income, bank statements, and identification — though it varies by situation and loan. We give you a clear checklist up front so nothing catches you by surprise, and we review everything with you before you sign.

Soy comprador por primera vez. ¿Pueden ayudarme? / I'm a first-time buyer — can you help?

Absolutely. We'll walk you through every step, from getting pre-approved to closing day, and answer every question — no matter how basic it feels. There's no such thing as a dumb question when it's the biggest purchase of your life.

¿Necesito ser ciudadano para comprar una casa? / Do I need to be a citizen to buy a home?

Homeownership is open to many buyers regardless of citizenship status, and there are lenders who work with a range of documentation. The details depend on your situation — we'll connect you with the right people and help you understand your options honestly.

¿Cuánto necesito para el enganche? / How much do I need for a down payment?

Often less than people assume. Minnesota has first-time buyer and down-payment-assistance programs, and loan options vary widely. We'll help you get a clear, realistic number before you start shopping.

¿Trabajan en los suburbios también? / Do you work in the suburbs too?

Yes — Minneapolis, St. Paul, and across the metro and western Wisconsin. Tell us where you're looking and we'll give you an honest read on the neighborhoods that fit your family and budget.
