Not a niche. The standard.

Plenty of agents will tell you they're welcoming. We put it in writing. As inclusive real estate agents in Minneapolis and across the Twin Cities, serving people who've too often been overlooked in housing isn't a side specialty for us — it's simply how we work with everyone.

We follow the letter and the spirit of fair housing law. No coded language, no steering, no quiet assumptions about who belongs where. Just honest guidance and an advocate who's genuinely in your corner.

Why we built it this way.

Four founders. Eighty years of Twin Cities real estate between us. We've seen how the industry can quietly leave people out — and we started Progressive to do the opposite, on purpose.

That's the whole idea: real estate done with fairness, dignity, and respect for everyone, every time. Not because it markets well, but because it's right.
