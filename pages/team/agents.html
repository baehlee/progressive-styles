Helping buyers & sellers since 2012

Jon Lopez

Agent

Jon's approach starts with something he's always valued in a salesperson: straight talk. Clear advice, no runaround, and the room to make your own decision in the end. He's been guiding people through their real estate moves since 2012, and he treats every one as a chance to genuinely connect — not just to close. Jon came to the U.S. from Colombia at age five and watched his parents chase the American Dream the hard way: working hard, getting a little further each year, from North Jersey to Minnetonka, where the family has been since 1997. That's the dream he's honored to help his own clients build now — a place of their own, and someone in their corner who'll shoot straight the whole way through.

Drew Wallis

Associate Broker

Drew brings a background most agents don't. Years as a teacher taught him to break a complicated thing down until it actually makes sense; years running a small business taught him how contracts and negotiations really work. He puts both to work on every transaction — especially for first-time buyers who need someone to explain the process and fight their corner. Originally from Arkansas, Drew and his family chose Minnesota on purpose: they wanted a community that believes everyone deserves equal treatment, and that a better future is worth working for. He's been helping people buy and sell for more than four years. Outside of work, you'll find him with his wife and daughter — usually mid-project, or learning something new.
