Meet the founders

Four founders. One standard.

Eighty years of Twin Cities real estate between us — and one shared belief: everyone deserves a place to belong. Press play to hear it from us, then let's talk about your move.

20 years · Referral-driven · Hablo Español

Rodolfo Trujillo

Co-Founder · Broker Associate

For more than 20 years, Rodolfo has built his business the way it should be built — on relationships, trust, and referrals. His rule is simple: treat people the way you'd want to be treated. Clients come back, and they send their families, because he makes a complicated process feel personal and even a little fun. He's known for sharp negotiation, an eye for detail, and staying calm when a deal gets complicated. Family is the center of his world — his wife Jen and their daughters remind him daily what home really means. He works in English and Spanish, and he's proud to help build a brokerage where everyone is treated with respect.

27 years · Relationship-first

Travis Callstrom

Co-Founder

Twenty-seven years in, Travis still believes real estate is about people, not paperwork. He's steady, thoughtful, and relationship-first — the kind of advisor who guides clients through every kind of life change and stays in their corner long after closing. He shares life with his husband, Bryan, and their two kids, and time together — often at their cabin in Wisconsin — keeps him centered. Travis helped build Progressive around a simple promise: that inclusive, equitable service isn't a niche, it's the standard. Serving LGBTQ+ families, and anyone who's ever wondered whether they'd be welcome, isn't a side mission for him. It's the mission.

Banking & mortgage lens

Joe Mack

Co-Founder

Joe came to real estate from the other side of the table — more than a decade in banking and the mortgage industry. That financial lens is his edge: he helps clients understand the numbers before they sign, so the biggest decision of their lives feels clear instead of overwhelming. He grew up on a dairy farm, where showing up early and working hard weren't optional, and he brings that same reliability to every client. Outside of work, Joe's world revolves around his wife, Penny, and their kids — usually somewhere up north, on a lake. Joe helped found Progressive on a belief in fairness and equal access for everyone.

23 years · Construction & value

Jake Miller

Co-Founder

Jake has spent more than 23 years in real estate, starting in mortgage in 2003 before earning his license in 2004. He knows financing, he knows value, and — thanks to a construction background — he knows what's really behind the walls. That means you buy the house, not the staging. Jake has worked through the housing crash, the COVID market, and plenty of personal curveballs, and it's made him the steady, straight-talking advisor clients lean on. No spin, no pressure — just clarity. Away from work, his greatest pride is his family, including his partner Samantha. Jake helped build Progressive because he believes real opportunity in housing should be open to everyone, full stop.
