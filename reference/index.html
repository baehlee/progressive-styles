The No Hate Pledge
"
To advance inclusive homeownership by providing equitable, informed, and client-centered real estate services — because everyone deserves a place to belong.
— Our Founding Mission · 2024
i.

Equal advocacy.

LGBTQ+ households, multilingual families, accessibility-conscious buyers, and first-generation homeowners get the same caliber of advisor — not a "specialist" treatment. The same four founders. The same standard.

ii.

Informed by design.

Two of our founders trained as mortgage lenders. We read your finances the way the bank does, then translate. No surprises at the closing table.

iii.

Client-centered, always.

We move at your pace, not the market's. We name when a house is wrong for you. We're paid when it closes — but we work like you're family, not commission.

How we work

Buy. Sell. Relocate.

Three paths, one team. Every transaction touches at least one of the four of us — sometimes all four, depending on what you need.

i. — buying

Find a place that fits your life, not the algorithm's idea of it.

We start with a real conversation about your finances (two of us trained as lenders) and what "home" actually means for you. Then we hunt.

  • Pre-offer financial coaching
  • Off-market and coming-soon access
  • Honest valuation, not just enthusiasm
  • Construction & condition walkthroughs
Buyer's path →
ii. — selling

Top dollar, on your timeline, without the theatrics.

Pricing built on actual comps, not seller flattery. Marketing that makes your home findable to buyers who actually want it.

  • Strategic pricing & comp analysis
  • Professional photo, video, and 3D tours
  • Targeted digital & MLS marketing
  • Negotiation by founders, not assistants
Seller's path →
iii. — relocating

Moving to the Twin Cities — or out of them — with a guide who's been here.

Neighborhoods, schools, commutes, communities. Especially if you're moving from somewhere that wasn't built to welcome you, we know where you'll feel at home.

  • Twin Cities neighborhood tours (virtual or in-person)
  • School & district counsel
  • LGBTQ+ & multicultural community guidance
  • Out-of-state agent referrals when you're leaving
